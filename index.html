Rules of the Game

You play as Owen Grady, the animal behaviorist who has been training Velociraptors at Jurassic World - a new park built on the vision of John Hammond and Dr. Henry Wu. Catastrophe strikes as Mt. Sibo has erupted. Ride with your Velociraptor, Blue, as you escape the island as it wrecks havoc on the island.

Use the SPACE BAR to jump over obstacles. Do not hit any obstacles if you want to escape the island.

Play against other players to achieve the high score
